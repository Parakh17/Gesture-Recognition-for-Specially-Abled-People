Sign Language Detection

What is Sign Language ?

The Sign language is a visual language used by the people with the speech and hearing disabilities for communication in their daily conversation activities. The goal of the project is to come up with an innovative solution for the deaf and hard of hearing community and develop an inclusive environment for them. The community is not able to express themselves and feel excluded at moments. The goal is further motivated to overcome the isolation and loneliness that is felt within the community. The main areas that are taken into consideration are the communication gap, dealing with the emergency crisis followed by the health and awareness section. For the communication section, an interface is made which takes gestures as input and converts them to text. Handling the emergency situations is one of the needs that need to be focussed upon in case of panic situations or crisis.

Objective

The purpose of Sign Language Recognition (SLR) systems is to provide an efficient and accurate way to convert sign language into text or voice has aids for the hearing impaired for example, or enabling very young children to interact with computers (recognizing sign language), among others.

Outcomes

This project is built from the dataset collected by us through webcam and we processed it to create the base of the project. In this we used mediapipe library that is efficient in detecting pose and body movements and can be used to identify different poses. The dataset collected is converted to numpy arrays which makes the training process faster and also images deal well in numpy arrays. Afterwards we built a LSTM and Dense neural network for the final prediction of our work. With success of the model real time predictions were done.
